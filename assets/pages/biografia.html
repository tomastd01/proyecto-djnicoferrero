biography

Nico Ferrero, is a DJ & Music Producer from Argentina.

He was born in 1983, in Caballito, Buenos Aires.

He began his path in music at the age of 12 studying classical guitar. During his adolescence he complemented his learning of musical composition in piano and singing and thus began to produce his first songs.

In 2004 he turned completely to electronic music, influenced by artists like Hernan Cattaneo, Sasha and John Digweed he was fascinated to see them live at memorable parties like Moonpark and Creamfields.

He studied to be a DJ at the Escuela Sonica in Buenos Aires. And through teachers like Hernan Paredes and Emiliano Folgar he became familiar with the world of music production.

In 2012 he released his first album called 'My Vegetarian Friends' through Hungary's Stella Fountain label.

Then and until 2015 he published his music on Bonzai Progressive (Belgium), Suffused music (Lithuania), Abstract Space (Russia), Massive Harmony (Argentina), Evoked Recordings (USA), 3xA Music (Russia), Soundteller Records (Poland), Revelación (Italy), Reelaux Digital (Hungary), Hidrógeno (Greece).

In 2015 and until 2017 he produced his own events, under the name of Mixing Green, together with a group of friends with whom they shared their passion for music in the famous basement of Recoleta Buenos Aires, Shamrock.

He made various B2B with producer friends and a duo called Tulum. Within this context, he played alongside local references of those years, such as Soundexile, Marcelo Vasami, Fernando Ferreyra, Jimmy Van M, Interaxxis and Dark Soul Project. And also with Rulo Lavigne and the French producer Nicolas Masseyeff, in Bahrain.

In 2019, he undertook his digital project called Producciones de Música Electrónica (pdmeplus.com), aimed at music producers and with the participation of leading professionals in the industry, such as Ernesto Romeo, Santiago Domingo Devia, Ulises Labaronnie, Nahuel Mijal, Gustavo Massone, Seph, Paula Os, Nicolas Morbelli , Santiago Villa, Alan Senderowitsch and Pablo Gil ; Arturia, La Siesta del FGauno, Estudio Cromo and Argenlive.

In 2022 he released his recent album on Food Por Music record label, Minor Label of Natura Viva (Italy) changing to a new identity to his artistic profile, Melodic Techno oriented.
